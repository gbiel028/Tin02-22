Olá Mundo!

The history of Brazil does not have a well-defined starting point. However, traditionally, there is a recurring dating of the arrival of the Portuguese with Pedro Álvares Cabral, on April 22, 1500, in the coastal region of what is now Bahia. This would then be the “discovery of Brazil”. However, it should be noted that this is the discovery of the Portuguese. Several ethnic groups already inhabited the territory that became Brazil long before any European landed there.
